Jidaar’s Quality Assurance, Safety & Health Policy

Jidaar is fully committed to development, in order to achieve clients’ expectations and satisfaction, by maintaining the principles of Quality Assurance, Safety & Health, and fulfills the requirements of the top management as per the standard specifications ISO 9001 : 2015 & OHSAS 18001 :2007 leading to continuous performance improvement.


Full commitment to the local and international standard specifications in the construction field.

Doing risk assessments by identifying, analyzing and ensuring all control measures have been taken to minimize injuries.

Doing all the analysis of any incident to reduce its effects and avoid its re- occurrence in the future.

Providing all safety and protections means and required precautions to emergency cases, and training all workers to follow the appropriate instructions to ensure their safety and environmental safety.

Training workers to improve their work skills.

Continuous improvement of quality management system, safety and occupational health through record, measurement and revision system.

Optimum use of resources and up-to-date equipment leading to continuous progress in work strategies and zero loss. - Training workers to improve their work skills.
